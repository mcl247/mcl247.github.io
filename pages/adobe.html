Adobe

Designing a Collaborative Ecosystem

Roles  //  Experience Design, User Research, Design Systems Thinking
Timeline: Summer 2018


Overview

I joined Adobe as an Experience Design intern on the Creative Cloud Assets and Collaboration team. Adobe design is going through change in terms of design systems thinking and collaboration between products, and CC Assets and Collaboration plays a key role in reimagining how collaboration works internally and externally.

My role this summer was to design a collaborative ecosystem and examine how different creatives within the creative environment collaborate on projects together. I had the opportunity to work with extremely talented people. Within Adobe, I collaborated with my design manager, design researchers, program managers, engineers, product managers, operation managers, workplace strategists and of course, other experience designers.

Unfortunately, the project is under NDA. Please contact me directly for more information!
