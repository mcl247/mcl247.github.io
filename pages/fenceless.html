Fenceless

Designing to foster neighborhood community

Roles  //  UI/UX Design
Timeline: September-December 2017


PROBLEM STATEMENT
Neighborhood relations are not as strong as they used to be. Neighbors no longer interact with one another on a personal level.

Overview


Community is what gives its members a sense of belonging and offers support to those in need. To help facilitate reconnecting these groups, I designed a solution that fosters personal relationships among members within the same local community.
