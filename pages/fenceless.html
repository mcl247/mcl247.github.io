Fenceless

Designing to foster neighborhood community

Roles  //  UI/UX Design
Timeline: September-December 2017 // Team: Cathy Liu, Eric Gendreau, Alexis Shore, Alex Fernandez, Vivian Jiang


PROBLEM STATEMENT
Neighborhood relations are not as strong as they used to be. Neighbors no longer interact with one another on a personal level.

Overview


Community is what gives its members a sense of belonging and offers support to those in need. To help facilitate reconnecting these groups, I designed a solution that fosters personal relationships among members within the same local community.


Goals


1. Increase frequency of interactions between neighbors.

2. Develop a sense of trust and belonging within the neighborhood.

3. Allow optionality for neighbors to interact.

4. Create a pleasant physical environment.

5. Create a sense of understanding amongst neighbors despite cultural differences.
