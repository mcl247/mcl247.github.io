CUSDiscover

Roles  //  Art Direction, Design Manager
view the website
Timeline: Ongoing - Spring 2018 //Team: Cathy Liu, Tony Liu, Kenneth Yeung, Yanis Park, Emily Chen, Alice Rhim, Michael Choe






Overview

A collection of “stories” of Cornell University Sustainable Design members in their academic and professional lives. CUSD related or not, we want to emphasize the breadth of skills our members have and the milestones they have accomplished. Whether that be a systems engineer's prototype, an environmental science & sustainability major’s research publication, or a designer’s case study, anything that exemplifies a CUSD member’s skills can be published on this site.
