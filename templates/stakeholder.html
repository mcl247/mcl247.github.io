PRESENTING CONTENT IN A PROFESSIONAL MANNER


Our stakeholders and partners help us make projects into reality. They are invaluable to us. We look to appeal to reliable stakeholders who ideally have existing positive relationships with the community, have missions that are aligned with the ones of CUSD, be open to creativity, and are willing to serve as mentors to us throughout the project.

My role in this project was to create a holistic package used to present both our past and current projects to our current and potential stakeholders. This features ten past projects and eight current projects. The hardest part about this project was figuring out what information to include and curating it in a way that is professional yet engaging to stakeholders.





What I Learned...


"A user interface is like a joke. If you have to explain it, it is not that good". My team-lead said this quote in the beginning of the year and it has stuck with me ever since. This quote inspired me to create designs with purpose that people will understand. This involves A LOT of in-depth research into understanding what the project is all about, who are the target audiences I am designing for, and what type of designs will be the most impactful. What I discovered was that knowing the purpose of my designs not only helps me design more impactful things, but it also makes the process of designing a whole lot more enjoyable.
