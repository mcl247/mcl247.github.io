❀ ABOUT KAPPA PHI LAMBDA ❀

Kappa Phi Lambda (ΚΦΛ) Sorority, Inc. is a multicultural, Asian-Interest (NOT Asian-exclusive) sorority founded nationally at Binghamton University in 1995. As one of the fastest growing Asian-Interest sororities in the nation, we strive to strengthen SISTERHOOD amongst women, provide SERVICE to our community, and promote CULTURAL DIVERSITY by educating individuals about Pan-Asian culture.

❀ ABOUT RUSH ❀

Rush is the two-week period during which YOU can get to know US :) Not sure what we're all about? Just come out to any of our events! All our events are FREE and non-binding.

Stop by, meet some sisters, and have some fun! And who knows, maybe you'll end up falling in love :)
